Need Help Identifying your Ghost?

Poltergeist:
Poltern meaning 'to make noise' and Geist meaning 'ghost' in German, Poltergeists are active ghosts who love to throw things, cause loud noises and flicker lights. Preferring to reside in ordinary homes, specifically high energy rooms such as the kitchen or bathroom. If your ghost is more active than most, it's likely you have a Poltergeist amidst your home.
Banshee:
Banshees are a female spirit from Irish folklore (where they're more commonly known as bean sĺdhe). They're easily identifiable from their wailing cry. They often appear as an older woman with long hair. Witnessing this ghost type can be seen as a bad omen and it's important that you log a report on this ghost type as soon as possible!
Spirit:
Non-corporeal ghosts that are born from unfinished business. This ghost type can be both human and animal! Generally harmless for the most part but reports can help us with our research so are still encouraged!

Need Help Identifying a Magical Creature?

Equine
The equine category encompasses anything resembling a Unicorn, Pegasus, Centaur and Kelpie. Any creature that falls within the Equidae family can be characterised by features such as single-hooves, a herbivorous diet (with the exception of Selkies), long heads and manes!
Fairy
Fairy Magical Creatures can be defined by their small, humanoid build. Creatures that fall under this category would be Pixies, Goblins and Sprites!
Feline
Similar to their non-magical brethren, Magical Felines inherit many of the same biological traits as their non-magical counterparts. Examples that fall within this group are Tressyms, Griffins, Chimeras, Displacer Beasts and Cat Lords.
